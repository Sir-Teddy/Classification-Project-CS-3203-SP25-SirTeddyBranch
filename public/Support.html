Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I enroll in a course?

Browse the courses page, select a course, and click the "Enroll Now" button.

How are AI evaluations scored?

Our AI system analyzes your work based on rubrics, comparing against standards and previous submissions.

Can I download my course materials?

Yes, most course materials have a download option available on the course page.
